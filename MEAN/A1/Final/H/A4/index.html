Welcome to Fergusson College

Fergusson College, established in 1885, is a renowned institution of higher education located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, it stands out for its academic excellence and has a rich history spanning over a century. The college offers a diverse array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines including Science, Arts, and Commerce.
In the Science stream, students can pursue subjects like Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science. The Arts faculty provides courses in English, History, Political Science, Sociology, and Philosophy, while the Commerce department includes programs in Accounting, Management, and Economics.
The college is equipped with modern facilities, including well-stocked libraries, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a comprehensive sports complex. For students requiring accommodation, there are hostels available, and a cafeteria caters to the dietary needs of both students and staff.
Fergusson College is known for its strong reputation in academia and research, with many of its alumni achieving prominence in various fields such as politics, literature, science, and business. The college also emphasizes extra-curricular activities, hosting cultural events, and supporting various student-run clubs and societies.
